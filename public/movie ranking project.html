The Best Movies According to Federica

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Inception

This is one of the most intriguing thrillers of all time.
With a constant leap between reality and dreams, it keeps you glued to the screen,
trying to figure out whether what you see is part of the conscious or the subconscious.

Harry Potter

Comprising 8 films, it is one of the most magical sagas of all time.

Shrek

A great animated film that brought about an evolution in the field of animation,
target audience, and a renewal in how fairy tales are told.
